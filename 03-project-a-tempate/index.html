Fields with flowers in an uninhabited village

Appearance:

White, almost transparent butterfly. The caterpillar is green, with a red head

Key Characteristics:

  • Flies
  • Changes colo
  • Turns into caterpillar
  • Turns back into butterfly

What do we know so far about the creature?

At first glance it seems like an ordinary butterfly that moves from flower to flower, but at the end of the day the butterfly becomes a caterpillar, which contradicts the well-known development of the butterfly. A certain flower helps to change into the primary appearance
